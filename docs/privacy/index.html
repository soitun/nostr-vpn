Nostr VPN Privacy Policy

Effective April 29, 2026

Nostr VPN is a private VPN and generic WireGuard exit-node utility, not a public VPN, anonymity, stealth, or consumer internet proxy service. It uses FIPS private mesh tunnels between devices and services you choose, and can route traffic through WireGuard exit nodes you configure.

Data Used to Operate the VPN

Nostr VPN processes the following data to create and maintain VPN connections:

  • your Nostr public key, device name, network membership, and peer list;
  • tunnel addresses, FIPS peer endpoints, and routing choices;
  • join requests, signed roster metadata, relay choices for FIPS discovery, mesh status, and connection state;
  • traffic counters and health information shown in the app.

Private keys and local configuration are stored on your device. Packet traffic inside the VPN tunnel is encrypted. When you use an exit node, FIPS relay/bridge path, Nostr relay, or peer-operated device, that operator receives the data needed to provide the service you asked to use.

How the Data Is Used

Nostr VPN uses VPN data only to operate the VPN features you configure, including discovery, pairing, routing, peer connectivity, and exit-node operation.

The developer does not sell VPN data and does not use or disclose VPN data to third parties for any purpose. The app only transmits the connection data needed to the peers, FIPS discovery/rendezvous relays, bridge paths, and exit nodes you choose to operate your VPN.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking. TestFlight and Apple platforms may provide crash reports or feedback if you choose to submit them through Apple.

Your Choices

You choose which networks, FIPS discovery/rendezvous relays, peers, routes, and exit nodes to use. You can remove peers, leave networks, stop the VPN tunnel, or delete the app to remove local app data from your device.
